# Final Insights – Data Driven Sales Strategy

## Customer Satisfaction
Higher income professions (Engineer, Manager, Doctor) show the highest product satisfaction (>4.5/5).
Mid income professions (Nurse, Teacher, Artist) show moderate satisfaction (4.2–4.5/5).
Low income group (Jobless) shows lowest satisfaction (~3.4/5).

This indicates purchasing power strongly influences perceived product value.

---

## Customer Segmentation
Customers can be grouped into three segments:
- Premium (High income)
- Mid (Medium income)
- Low (Low income)

Income level explains most of the variation in satisfaction and adoption.

---

## Discount Impact
Summer Sale:
- Premium: +54%
- Mid: +11%

Winter Sale:
- Premium: −49%
- Mid: −65%

Low segment:
- No meaningful response to discounts

Conclusion: discounts are effective mainly for customers already willing to purchase.

---

## Product Preferences
Ratings are similar across colors.
Red and Blue products sell more in Premium and Mid segments.

---

## Strategy Recommendation
- Focus marketing on Premium and Mid segments
- Keep Summer discount campaigns
- Avoid Winter sales campaigns
- Limit discounts for Low segment
- Prioritize Red and Blue product variants
